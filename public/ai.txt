## The Problem We Solve

### People Don't Browse Anymore

Modern customers have changed. They want answers now, and if they have to wait or search for information, they leave.

**Key Issues:**
- **People are in a hurry** - Customers don't browse anymore. They want an answer now. If they have to wait or look for it, they'll leave.
- **If it looks bad, they leave** - If your site looks old or slow, people think your business is old or slow. You lose the sale before they even read a word.
- **One page doesn't fit everyone** - You shouldn't send every customer to the same front door. We build specific pages for specific goals.

### The 30-Second Decision Window

Visitors decide whether to buy or leave in just 30 seconds. During this critical window, they're asking three questions:

1. **What do you sell?** - 88% of visitors leave if they can't figure this out in 5 seconds
2. **Do you have what I want?** - Cluttered sites with 40+ elements convert 50% less than clear sites
3. **How do I buy it?** - Every extra second of confusion costs 7% in conversions

**Our Goal:** Answer all 3 questions instantly. We build sites so clear that visitors know what you sell, find what they want, and buy—all in under 30 seconds.

**Performance Difference:**
- Confusing Site: 88% bounce rate, 1.5% conversion
- Clear Site: 12% bounce rate, 3.8% conversion
